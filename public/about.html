GDG Allahabad
Google Developer Groups (GDGs) are for developers who are interested in Google's developer technology; everything from the Android, Chrome, Drive, and Google Cloud platforms, to product APIs like the Cast API, Maps API, and YouTube API.

A GDG can take many forms -- from just a few people getting together to watch our latest video, to large gatherings with demos and tech talks, to events like code sprints and hackathons. However, at the core, GDGs are focused on developers and technical content, and the core audience should be developers.

What now? Visit the directory to see if there's a local meetup in your area. Feel free to contact the organizers to find out more. If there's no chapter near you, consider starting one!

Google Developers Group, Allahabad is the group that represents the Developers, Designers and Enthusiasts in and around the region. It provides a platform for all of you to get to know about the various Google Technologies. You can learn how these technologies will help you in your everyday development needs and how is it gonna improve your performance and give you boost in your tasks. Its a great place to learn, share, develop and to be.

Take part in the various community activities and discover the most out of your fellow developers and Google.
